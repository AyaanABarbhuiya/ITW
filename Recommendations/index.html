Anime Recommendations

  1. Code Geass: Hangyakusha no Lelouch - Can be heavy at times but is genuinely one of the best I've seen
  2. Serial Experiments Lain - Can go over your head at times but is really an interesting commentary on the Internet
  3. Black Clover
    4. -Pretty lacklustre as compared to the rest but it has one of the greatest plot progressions I've seen.
  4. Fate/Unlimited Blade Works-as much as I ADORE the Fate/Stay Night route, anime-wise UBW is objectively better.
  5. One Piece-An amazing piece of fiction with applausable worldbuilding and well-manangement of plot
  6. Dororo To Hyakkimaru (2019)
    7. - Better than the OG. Plus, Dororo is such an adorable girl!
  7. Kakumeiki Valvrave- Don't enquire me about S1E10. lots of idiotic plot holes but still enjoyable (except the ending)
